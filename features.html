02 · Account command center

Sessions stay on your PC.

Sign in through the official Roblox page. Fleet captures the resulting session, encrypts it with Windows DPAPI, and only decrypts it in the main process when minting a single-use launch ticket.

  • No passwords handled by Fleet
  • 2FA and captcha supported
  • Robux, Premium, avatar, and presence
